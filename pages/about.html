who we are?

Dharmesh Solanki Projects has provided the services of architecture & interior design for more than a decade and are considered amongst the   interior designers in Rajkot . As a result, the firm has witnessed how the requirement of various interior & exterior products have increased and due to improper management & lesser amount of supply, this need has remained unfulfilled for so long now. For bridging this gap, along with interior services the firm has explored this field of providing interior as well as exterior products. For interior products, the firm owns its exclusive showroom. For exploring exterior products, facade system is introduced. With help of our extensive field experience and knowledge, these facade systems are developed not only for the elevation with good aesthetic but also for efficient utility and prevention from surrounding weather. Every system has its own exclusive qualities.
